This website is the marketing and documentation site for the open-source
WebReaper project. We keep data collection to a minimum.

## What we collect

- **Anonymous analytics.** Aggregate page-view metrics with no personally
  identifying information.
- **Waitlist email (optional).** If you join the WebReaper Cloud waitlist, we
  store the email address you provide solely to contact you about availability.

## What we do not do

We do not sell your data, and we do not run third-party advertising trackers.
